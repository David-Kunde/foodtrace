How It Works

1

Data Collection

IoT sensors collect critical data points throughout the food production chain, from soil conditions to temperature during transport.

2

Blockchain Verification

Each data point is verified and stored on our secure blockchain ledger, creating an immutable record that cannot be altered.

3

Smart QR Generation

A unique QR code is generated for each product batch, linking to its complete history and journey information.

4

Consumer Access

Consumers scan the QR code at point of purchase to access the complete journey of their food in an intuitive visual timeline.
